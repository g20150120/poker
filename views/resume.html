About This Website

I build this personal website using a template from StyleShout. I used Node as server, jade as view engine, a domain from GoDaddy, and a vultr VPS to host it. This website is mobile-compatible but is best viewed in desktop Chrome or Safari. CLICK HERE for pictures in the website in their original resolution.

I came up with the logo on the left on my own. Its shape of sharp-edged square implies concision and order. Its color in light orange denotes the energetic and passionate part of me. 0 and 1 represents the binary world of computer science and are the most important 'identity' in mathematics; A and Z represents my initials, Alex (Junchen) Zhao, and also somewhat represents human language, as the first and last of alphabet.

I would like to use this website to share something about me. This project is also a trial to develop and deploy a somewhat well-organised, good-looking, and fully operational website from scratch. Image or file transmission might be inefficient or even ineffective sometimes, but please be patient.
