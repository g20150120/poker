About This Website

I built this personal website using a template from StyleShout. I used Node as server, jade as view engine, a domain from GoDaddy, and a vultr VPS as the host. This website is mobile-compatible but is best viewed in desktop Chrome or Safari. CLICK HERE for pictures in the website in their original resolution.

The logo on the left is my own design. Its shape of sharp-edged square implies concision and order. Its color in light orange represents my energy and passion. 0 and 1 are the most important 'identity' in mathematics and represents the binary world in computer science; A and Z represents my initials, Alex (Junchen) Zhao, and are also the first and last of the English alphabet.

I would like to use this website to share something about me. This project is also a trial to develop and deploy a somewhat well-organised, good-looking, and fully operational website from scratch. Image or file transmission might be inefficient or even ineffective sometimes, but please be patient HAHA :)

High Frequency Trading Strategies

HFT Strategy on Noise Return of Stock Index Futures • Summer 2019

I did this research during my internship in Beijing iVolution Capital. It was inspired by Epps Effect and the lead-lag relationship between hushen 300 stock index and its derivative, hushen 300 stock index futures. I tried to use basis as a reference to capture great deviations of futures price from its value and trade futures accordingly.
